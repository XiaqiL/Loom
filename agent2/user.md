# User Profile

## Brand Overview
- Brand name: Luma & Thread
- Founder: Emily Zhang
- Business type: DTC fashion and lifestyle accessories
- Primary sales channels: Shopify, Etsy, Instagram Shop
- Base market: United Kingdom
- Secondary markets: United States, Canada, Australia

## Brand Positioning
Luma & Thread is a modern lifestyle brand focused on minimalist, soft-toned, giftable fashion accessories for women aged 22–38. The brand emphasizes wearable design, comfort, and aesthetically pleasing packaging.

## Target Audience
- Women aged 22–38
- Interested in gifting, fashion accessories, and soft neutral aesthetics
- Frequent online shoppers on Etsy, Instagram, and Shopify-powered stores
- Values visual branding, quality feel, and trendy but timeless design

## Product Focus
Current core categories include:
- satin scrunchies
- linen tote bags
- embroidered cosmetic pouches
- lightweight scarves

## Pricing Strategy
- Mid-range pricing
- Target gross margin: 60%+
- Products should feel premium but still accessible
- Bundles and gift sets are encouraged

## Brand Style
- Visual tone: soft, minimal, feminine, modern
- Main colors: ivory, blush pink, sage green, beige
- Preferred materials: satin, linen, cotton, canvas
- Packaging style: clean, giftable, eco-conscious

## Business Goals
- Improve pricing strategy based on market benchmarks
- Identify strong competitors across major platforms
- Expand best-selling product lines
- Improve product positioning and rebranding direction
- Track revenue performance across shops over time

## Notes for Agent
- Focus on products that are visually giftable and trend-friendly
- Prioritize insights relevant to Etsy, Shopify, Instagram, Google Shopping, and Amazon
